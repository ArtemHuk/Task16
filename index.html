Why use both butter and olive oil to make fried potatoes? It adds complexity and nuance to the flavor: you can taste the difference! The butter is savory and rich, and the olive oil adds a subtle nuttiness. Alex and I started using butter and olive oil as a combo a few years ago, and we love pulling out the “olive oil and butter” trick to make things taste over the top incredible. If you eat vegan, you can just substitute the butter with additional olive oil.
